About Us

Who We Are

SAGE (Sustainabile Action for Green Education) is a pioneering organization dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainable education. Founded on the principles of ecological stewardship and community empowerment, we work tirelessly to create innovative solutions for environmental challenges.

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to environmental education combines hands-on learning, community engagement, and innovative solutions to create lasting impact.

Educational Programs

Comprehensive sustainable education for schools, communities, and organizations.

  • Interactive workshops and seminars
  • Custom curriculum development
  • Teacher training programs
  • Online learning resources

Community Outreach

Engaging communities through events, campaigns, and hands-on projects.

  • Tree planting initiatives
  • Clean-up campaigns
  • Recycling programs
  • Environmental awareness events

Conservation Projects

Direct action projects that protect and restore natural environments.

  • Habitat restoration
  • Wildlife protection
  • Sustainable agriculture
  • Water conservation
